# Quant Systems Lab

Quant Systems Lab is a tested Python research platform for quantitative finance. Its flagship research direction is queue-aware market making evaluated on real event-level order-book data with explicit chronology, latency, queue uncertainty, fees, inventory risk, and adverse selection.
